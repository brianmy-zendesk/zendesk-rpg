How to Play

  • Arrow Keys or WASD to move in the overworld
  • Walk near Milton and press ENTER to talk
  • Answer questions correctly to defeat bosses
  • Each wrong answer costs you a life (stapler)
  • Answer quickly - enemies attack after 15 seconds!
  • Press P or the Coffee Break button to pause the timer
  • Defeat all bosses across 3 levels to win
